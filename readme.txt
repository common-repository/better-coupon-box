Create coupon popups to collect email subscribers, social followers, give away coupons. Exit-intent popups to prevent abandoned carts.

== Description ==

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2aqSYixiYH8

This plugin is built to seamlessly connect your WordPress website to our <b><a href="https://beeketing.com/better-coupon-box?utm_channel=applisting&utm_medium=woolisting&utm_fromapp=cbox" target="_blank">Better Coupon Box</a></b> app on Beeketing platform. All features are configured and managed in our server, so the plugin is most lightweight as possible and takes minimum resources from your website.

When customers enter your store, a beautiful welcome popup will display, asking them to subscribe email or follow your social media accounts (Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, Pinterest, Google+) in return for a coupon code.

The eCommerce industry sees an average of 68% customers abandoning their cart right at cart page! Want to combat the cart abandonment rate for your store? Build powerful Exit-intent Popups with this extension to prevent any customers from leaving your site without completing purchase.

= HIGHLIGHTS: =

*  Grow email list by 400%+ per month and boost social followers
*  Increase sales by motivating new customers to start buying with a coupon code
*  Prevent cart abandonment with exit-intent popups
*  Design beautiful popups that match your store's look & feel

= SOCIAL INTEGRATION: =

Add these social icons on the popup, visitors click the icon to instantly like your social media pages: Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, Pinterest, Google+. You can remove any social icons if you don't want to collect followers.

= EMAIL SUBSCRIPTION: =

Add a small email subscription box on the popup and an outstanding Call-to-Action button to encourage visitors to subscribe email. You can remove this box if you don't want to collect emails.

= EMAILS AUTO-SYNC: =

Connect the extension with many external Email Service Providers to auto-sync collected emails into your email marketing list. We support many pouplar ESPs: MailChimp, Constant Contact, GetResponse, Aweber, iContact, Campaign Monitor, Klavyio,...

= EXIT-INTENT POPUPS: =

Easily create powerful exit-intent popups in 1 click. Exit-intent technology detects when your customers are about to leave the site by following their mouse's movement, thus display a popup right before they leave to catch their attention. You can use exit-intent popups to prevent abandoned carts at cart page and combat the 68% cart abandonment rate.

This feature works a bit differently on mobile: when customers leave a web page, exit-intent popups will show up the next time they come back.

= MINIMIZED ICON ON SITE CORNER: =

When customers close popup, it will be minimized into a small icon on the site corner. Customers can click on the icon to manually open the popup anytime they want. You can set the position of this icon where apropriate on your website. You can hide the icon so each customer can see the popup only once.

= MULTIPLE POPUPS: =

You can create many popups to run on different pages, target different groups of users, at the same time. There is no limitation on how many popups you can run at the same time

= LIMIT AUDIENCE: =

If you want to save marketing cost on the coupons, or target specific groups of visitors, we have options for you to limit audience who can see the popups, or limit pages on which popups display.

Depending on your campaign goal, you can:

*  Show popups to all visitors, new visitors only, or returning customers only.
*  Show popups on homepage, the second page they navigate, cart page, collection pages, or product pages only.

= SCHEDULE TIME: =

You can schedule a start date or/and end date for your popups.

= CUSTOMIZE POPUP THEMES: =

100% customizable coupon popup theme that can perfectly match your store’s look & feel. We have prepared many templates which you can upload images as background. Skilled at CSS/HTML? Play with your creativity and design your own coupon popup with our full CSS/HTML custom option.

= HOLIDAY THEMES COLLECTION: =

Holiday season is the best sales time of the year! We want your store to be colorful, merry, and beautiful on the holiday season, thus we pre-design many holiday themes to help you decorate your store, impress your customers and grow your sales.

The collection includes themes for Halloween, Thanksgiving, Black Friday, Cyber Monday, Christmas, New Year, Valentines,...

= RESPONSIVE ON DESKTOP & MOBILE: =

Popups are designed to be beautifully responsive on any desktop screens and mobile devices (tablets and smart phones). You can turn off popups on mobile devices.

= TRANSLATION: =

Default popups are in English. We support text translation to any languages.
